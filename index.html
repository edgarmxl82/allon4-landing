FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is the All on 4 dental implants procedure? +

The All on 4 technique is a revolutionary full mouth reconstruction solution that uses four titanium implants to support a full, permanent arch of teeth. It's a minimally invasive procedure offered at our dental clinic in Los Algodones, designed to give you a stable, beautiful smile quickly.

Why is it so much cheaper in Los Algodones, Mexico? +

Dental tourism in Mexico allows us to offer the same high-quality materials and care as in the U.S. but at a lower cost. This is due to lower overhead, lab costs, and living expenses. We pass these savings directly to you without compromising on quality.

Is it safe to get dental work in Mexico? +

Absolutely. Los Algodones is known as 'Molar City' and is a hub for dental tourism. Our clinic uses state-of-the-art technology and our dentists in Mexico are highly trained and experienced, often with U.S. certifications. We prioritize patient safety and satisfaction.

How do I get started with the free consultation? +

It's simple! Just click any of the 'Get My Free Consultation' buttons on this page and fill out the form. Our team will contact you to schedule a convenient time for your free online diagnosis and answer all your questions.

How long does the All on 4 procedure take? +

The surgical placement of the four implants is typically completed in just one day. You'll leave with a temporary set of teeth, and after a healing period of a few months, we will place your permanent, final bridge.

What is the recovery like? +

Recovery is generally faster and less painful than traditional full mouth implant methods. Most patients experience some swelling and discomfort for a few days, which can be managed with medication. We will provide detailed aftercare instructions to ensure a smooth recovery.
